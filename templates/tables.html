Overview Analysis

As we can see, both players and teams have statistics that can make up an entire explanation about their performance and keys to success. In order to better determine which team could be deemed as “the best” performing, statistics include analysis by yards (during offensive and defensive plays) which we consider to be fundamental to execute any pass, interception or touchdown. A team’s performance can be measured as a whole rather than the individual sum of its players, therefore a player’s overall gameplay may not be representative of his team. With this in mind, the analysis that we carried out reveals that the played yards show the impact of a team’s strategy and a player’s decisions. Best performing players can be identified as those with the highest yards in different plays (Passing, Rushing, Receiving) throughout the selected years (2016-2020)*.

For the team performance, the selected measures were scoring (points), possession time (minutes) and penalties occurred. We found out that teams with the highest time possession were those more prone to both achieve higher scores and get touchdowns., and also to incur in penalties. Past performance does not assure future events. The aforementioned findings get us to the conclusion that team performance is not directly related to each player alone. Even though the information here present helps us understand how have the past events been, it is important to always include further information such as seasons, player sentiment, changes in coaching or funding, etc.
